Legs

    Leg workouts are incredibly beneficial for overall fitness and health. By regularly performing leg exercises, you can see numerous benefits, such as improved hormone production, reduced risk of injury, and increased muscle growth. Leg workouts can stimulate the release of large amounts of hormones. Working your legs helps to produce hormones such as cortisol, testosterone, and human growth hormone

Chest

    Chest exercises can help improve your upper body strength and muscle endurance, which can assist in performing everyday activities such as carrying heavy objects or pushing heavy doors. Additionally, chest workouts can help improve your posture, reduce the risk of shoulder and neck injuries, and enhance athletic performance in sports that require upper body strength. Furthermore, chest workouts can help to increase overall muscle mass, which can boost metabolism and aid in weight management.

Back

    Developing a strong back is vital for enhancing one's overall quality of life, as it enables you to perform daily tasks with greater ease and helps prevent injuries. It's also worth noting that the term "back" is not a specific body part in it itself but rather the region between the base of the neck and the top of the pelvis. Back workouts can provide a variety of benefits, including improving posture, reducing back pain, and enhancing overall strength and muscle definition.
